## Focus

### Overview
---

**Focus** is a spell that grants the user mana based on certain conditions:
* If the user is at their maximum capacity of mana, their mana capacity will increase by 50
* Otherwise, their mana will be increased by 50. 
  - If adding 50 goes over their capacity, their mana amount will not overflow and will instead be set to their current max capacity.

The most amount of mana a user can have is 500. Once this amount is reached, the maximum mana capacity cannot be increased.

### Usage
---
The Focus spell is drawn with an M with a circle around it.

This spell costs zero mana to cast; however it has a ten second cooldown.

Upon casting the spell, the user gains an aura. The color of this aura can be changed by using a command in chat:

* `/setauracolor ffffff`
  - `ffffff` is a hex color representing white. This can be changed to any hex color.

After ten seconds, the aura will go away which also indicates that the spell is ready to be used again.

Focus is an essential spell to learn and master, especially since it cannot be saved onto a spell card for instant access.
